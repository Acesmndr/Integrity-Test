Integrity
Integrity (derived from a Latin adjective word ‘Integer’) can be defined as the inner sense of completeness referring to the qualities such as honesty and consistency of a character. It’s the quality of being honest and having strong moral principles; moral uprightness.
What is integrity test?
Integrity test is basically a quiz questionnaire, testing the integrity values in one. Transforming regular Q/A into a game in which one can score points and share to social medias like Facebook & Twitter, Integrity Test aims to create awareness about the moral values & integrity in mass.
Scoring Criteria
  • 2 points for Correct answer at first try
  • Only one points for correct answer after it’s been answered wrong once
  • And the game proceeds
Why was it developed?
Integrity Test was a part in Integrity Ideathon 2 as it secured the position 1st RunnerUp in the event. Ideathon 2 was organized by Integrity Action in partnership with YoungInnovations.
